Mozilla Thunderbird

  1. Open Thunderbird. In the menu bar go to File → New → Existing Mail Account (or click Set Up an Existing Email Account on the home screen).
  2. Enter your name, email address {{.Email}}, and password, then click Continue.
  3. Thunderbird will try auto-detection. If it fails, click Configure manually and enter:
Incoming (IMAP)Outgoing (SMTP)
Server{{.IMAPHost}}{{.SMTPHost}}
Port993587
ConnectionSSL/TLSSTARTTLS
Auth methodNormal passwordNormal password
Username{{.Email}}{{.Email}}
  1. Click Done. If you see a certificate warning, click Confirm Security Exception (expected for self-signed certificates).
  2. Your inbox will appear in the left sidebar under your email address.

Microsoft Outlook (Desktop)

  1. Open Outlook. Go to File → Add Account.
  2. Enter your email address {{.Email}} and click Connect.
  3. If Outlook fails auto-setup, select IMAP from the account type list and enter the incoming settings:
IMAP Server{{.IMAPHost}}
IMAP Port993 (SSL)
SMTP Server{{.SMTPHost}}
SMTP Port587 (STARTTLS)
Username{{.Email}}
  1. Enter your password when prompted and click Connect.
  2. If a certificate warning appears, click Accept or Yes to trust the server certificate.
  3. Click Done. Your account will appear in the folder pane.
Outlook 365 / New Outlook: Go to Settings → Accounts → Add account → Other and fill in the same IMAP/SMTP settings above.

Apple Mail (macOS / iOS)

macOS:
  1. Open Mail. Go to Mail → Add Account and select Other Mail Account….
  2. Enter your name, email {{.Email}}, and password. Click Sign In.
  3. Mail will fail auto-discovery — fill in manually:
Account TypeIMAP
Incoming Server{{.IMAPHost}}
Outgoing Server{{.SMTPHost}}
  1. Click Sign In. If prompted about a certificate, click Connect.
iOS / iPadOS: Go to Settings → Mail → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Mail Account. Enter the same details and select IMAP when asked.

Gmail (fetch & send as)

Gmail can fetch your mail and let you send as your address. Note: Gmail fetches via POP3, not IMAP — emails are downloaded and removed from the server.

Step 1 — Fetch incoming mail (POP3)
  1. In Gmail, go to Settings → See all settings → Accounts and Import.
  2. Under Check mail from other accounts, click Add a mail account.
  3. Enter {{.Email}} and click Next.
  4. Choose Import emails from my other account (POP3) and click Next.
  5. Fill in:
POP Server{{.IMAPHost}}
Port995 (SSL)
Username{{.Email}}
  1. Check Always use a secure connection (SSL), then click Add Account.
Step 2 — Send as your address
  1. Gmail will ask if you want to send mail as {{.Email}} — click Yes.
  2. Fill in the SMTP details:
SMTP Server{{.SMTPHost}}
Port587
Username{{.Email}}
ConnectionTLS
  1. Click Add Account. Gmail will send a confirmation code to your address — retrieve it from this webmail and paste it in.
