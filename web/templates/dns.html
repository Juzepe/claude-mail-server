Verification & Testing

1
Check DNS propagation — Use dnschecker.org or run: dig MX {{.Domain}}
2
Test email deliverability — Send a test email to mail-tester.com to check your score.
3
Verify DKIM — Run: dig TXT mail._domainkey.{{.Domain}}
4
Check SPF — Run: dig TXT {{.Domain}} and look for the v=spf1 record.
5
Check PTR (rDNS) — Ask your hosting provider to set a reverse DNS record pointing your server IP to {{.MailHost}}. This is critical for avoiding spam classification.
